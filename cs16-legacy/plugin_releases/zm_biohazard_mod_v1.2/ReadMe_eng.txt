------Classes Description--------------------------------

1. Stalker (Speed+), the health of 180, runs faster, and people jump a little higher (especially not noticeable).
2. FastStalker (Speed++), the health of 140, runs even faster than Stalker, jumping a little higher above people too.
3. Hulk (Strong++ Speed-), health of 300, go slower humans.
4. FastHulk (Strong+), 240 health, jump a little higher than humans.
5. SlowHulk (Strong+ Speed- Gravity-), health 200, jumping to 2 times higher than humans, go slower humans.медленней людей.
6. Leaper (Gravity-), health 160, jumping to 2 times higher than humans, fast run.
7. FastLeaper (Gravity-- Speed+), 130 health, jumps 3 times higher than people, fast run.
8. Gonome (Regeneration+), 170 health, quickly regenerates (restore health), fast run.

Attention! The following classes are visible only if you enter /class in chat, and then press 9.

9 1. FastGonome (Regeneration+++ Speed+), 140 health, very quickly regenerates, run faster people.
9 2. Alien (Regeneration+ Speed+ Gravity-), health 135, regenerates quickly, runs fast, jumps 2 times higher than people (a model from the movie "Alien").
9 3. Macara (Regeneration+ Gravity-), health 170, regenerates quickly, jumps 2 times higher than people.
9 4. Slum (Regeneration+++ Gravity- Speed++), health 95, very quickly regenerates, jumps 2 times higher than people, runs as a FastStalker.
